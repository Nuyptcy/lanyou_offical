Description

The flavor of Kvarøy Arctic salmon reflects the waters in which they are raised. These waters are Arctic cool and clear, with a deep-fjord current that keeps the water fresh and helps exercise the salmon, reducing fat levels and giving this beautiful fish a pristine, clean flavor, with a slight brininess of the sea.
No antibiotics, chemicals, or hormones.
